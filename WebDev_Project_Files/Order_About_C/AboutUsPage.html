Our Story

Take it Cheasy began its journey in 2017 when Eshwar, Jaspreet, and Amritjot decided to turn their shared love for cheese into a culinary adventure. Bonded by their fond memories of cheesy snacks and lazy afternoons spent together, they set out to create a haven for cheese lovers everywhere.

Starting with just a small food truck, Take it Cheasy quickly gained a loyal following for its creative takes on classic comfort foods. From gooey grilled cheese sandwiches to decadent macaroni and cheese, every dish was a celebration of cheese in all its glorious forms.

In 2020, Take it Cheasy took a bold step forward and opened a cozy café where people would gather to indulge in their cheesy cravings and unwind from the hustle and bustle of everyday life.

With a commitment to using the freshest ingredients, Take it Cheasy quickly became known for its high-quality, locally sourced cheese and farm-fresh produce.
